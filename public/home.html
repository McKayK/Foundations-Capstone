Moment Rings

Hello my name is David. Moment Rings began as a hobby in my garage, making memorable gifts for my family. I soon developed a passion and desire to help others commemorate and celebrate those same memorable moments in their own lives.

I’ve incorporated unusual and unique materials that represented something special to my clients, such as: wedding flowers, loved one’s ashes, sand from a special beach, dinosaur bones, antlers, or a favorite stone or metal.

Moment Rings is all about capturing the important, but often fleeting moments in your life. Life passes too quickly. Let me help you preserve the memories in a quality custom ring to enjoy and pass on to future generations.
